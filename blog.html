Some thoughts about learning HTML

October, 25 2015

This is not my first time writing a website in HTML, although it has been quite a long time. I first started learning html more than 15 years ago when I was in 6th grade. My mom was creating a website, and I wanted to have one too! She got me started with the basics, and I learned a lot on my own by reading online tutorials on how to do lots of fancy things like adding music and colors and pop-ups. As I got older I became interested on other things, and I haven't written any HTML since then.

It is really fun and exciting now to come back and learn everything all over again. Some things are surprisingly still familiar, however a lot has changed since 1998. I am looking forward to learning more and having fun along the way. Come back and check out my blog to learn about my journey.
